## PART I : SECTION (I) - GENERAL

## Government  Notifications

## THE  CONSTITUTION  OF  THE  DEMOCRATIC  SOCIALIST  REPUBLIC  OF  SRI  LANKA

## Notification

MAITHRIPALA SIRISENA, President.

Presidential Secretariat, Colombo 01, 18th January, 2015.
